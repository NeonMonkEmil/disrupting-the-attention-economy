# Why is this different from ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Meta?

The most common question about the Sovereign Discovery Agent is: *Don't ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Meta already have all this data about me?*

On the surface, yes. In practice, no. And the difference is everything.

There are three fundamental structural differences between what the incumbents do and what the Sovereign Discovery Agent does.

## 1. They optimize for their business, not yours.

ChatGPT is a product built by a company that needs to generate revenue. OpenAI has commercial relationships with Microsoft, enterprise clients, and increasingly with advertisers and partners. When ChatGPT recommends a tool or a service, there is no guarantee that recommendation is the best one for you. It is the best one that passes through the filter of OpenAI's commercial interests. 

Meta is even more explicit: every piece of data Meta holds about you exists for one purpose, to sell it to advertisers. The data is not working for you. It is working against you.

The Sovereign Discovery Agent has one client: you. The business model is a credit system where you pay for compute. There is no advertiser. There is no partner whose product needs to be surfaced. The agent's only incentive is to find the best result, because that is the only thing that makes you refill your credits.

## 2. Their data is held centrally and used against you.

When Meta knows you are pregnant before you have told your family, that information goes into a targeting profile that is sold to diaper brands and baby formula companies. When ChatGPT learns your preferences through your conversations, that data is stored on OpenAI's servers and used to train future models. You do not own it. You cannot delete specific parts of it. You cannot see it.

The Taste Graph lives on your device. The raw data never leaves. The company running the Sovereign Discovery Agent cannot read your preferences, cannot sell them, and cannot use them to train anything. You have an Amnesia Button that deletes any node in the graph instantly. This is not a privacy policy. It is a technical architecture that makes it physically impossible for the company to misuse your data.

## 3. They are search engines with memory. This is an agent with judgment.

Perplexity answers questions. ChatGPT answers questions. They are reactive. You ask, they respond. They do not proactively monitor your behaviour, build a multi-dimensional model of your taste, and then autonomously go out into the world to find things before you even know you want them.

The Sovereign Discovery Agent is not waiting for a query. The Monitor Agent is continuously running, observing, and updating the Taste Graph. When you do ask a question, the agent is not starting from scratch. It is drawing on months or years of accumulated, highly specific knowledge about who you are. And crucially, it can act autonomously: booking, paying, negotiating, and completing transactions on your behalf without you having to be present.

## The Bottom Line

ChatGPT knows what you asked it. Meta knows what you clicked on. The Sovereign Discovery Agent knows who you are. And unlike the other two, it is legally, technically, and commercially incapable of using that knowledge against you.
