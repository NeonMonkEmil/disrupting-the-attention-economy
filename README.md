# The Sovereign Discovery Agent

> **The Idea File for Disrupting the Attention Economy**
> 
> *In the agentic era, the scarcity of building has hit zero. The new scarcity is knowing what to build and why. This repository contains the complete blueprint for dismantling the Attention Economy. It is free to read, free to fork, and free to build.*

## The One-Line Pitch

A personal AI agent that knows you better than any algorithm ever could, and uses that knowledge to find exactly the right restaurant, book, song, computer, or account to follow, without a single ad influencing the result.

## The Problem It Solves

Every time you search Google, scroll Instagram, or browse Amazon, the result you see is not the best result for you. It is the result that either paid the most to be there, or that an algorithm calculated would keep you engaged the longest. The internet's discovery mechanism has been captured by three companies whose business model depends on your attention, not your satisfaction.

The Sovereign Discovery Agent is built on a single premise: your AI agent should work exclusively for you.

## What the User Actually Experiences

You download the app. You get a small balance of free credits to start. You open a chat and ask it something like:

*"Find me a restaurant in Stockholm for a first date. I like places that feel intimate but not stuffy, natural wine, and good fish."*

The agent does not Google it. It does not show you sponsored listings. It queries open databases, reads reviews, cross-references reservation availability, and returns two or three specific recommendations with a clear explanation of why each one matches your taste. You pick one. The agent notes which one you chose and why it resonated.

That choice becomes a data point in your **Taste Graph**.

## The Taste Graph

The Taste Graph is the heart of the product. It is a living, local map of your preferences that lives entirely on your own device, encrypted, and never sent to any server in raw form.

It is not a list of things you say you like. It is built from what you actually do. Every choice you make, every recommendation you accept or reject, every article you linger on, every song you replay, every purchase you complete, the Monitor Agent observes all of it silently and updates the graph. Over time, it learns that your preference for a certain type of restaurant is mathematically connected to your taste in furniture, which is connected to the kind of music you listen to on Sunday mornings.

The longer you use it, the more accurate it becomes. After six months, the agent does not just find what you asked for. It finds things you did not know you wanted.

This is what Google, Meta, and Amazon structurally cannot build. Their business model requires showing you ads. An agent that knows you this well and acts purely in your interest is an existential threat to their revenue.

## How You Pay

You load credits into the app the same way you top up a prepaid card. You pay with Apple Pay or a credit card. You get a small free balance on signup to experience the product before spending anything.

Behind the scenes, your credits are backed by stablecoins on a fast blockchain. When the agent spends compute or pays for data, the cost is deducted from your balance in real-time. You can see exactly what each task cost you. There is no flat subscription, no hidden fees, and no advertising.

*(Note: As of April 2026, Stripe has announced native infrastructure for exactly this model through their Agentic Commerce Suite, which means the payment layer can be built on top of existing, regulated financial infrastructure rather than custom crypto plumbing).*

## Why You Cannot Leave

After a year of use, your Taste Graph contains thousands of data points about who you are and what you value. The agent has learned things about your preferences that you have never consciously articulated. Going back to a generic Google search at that point would feel like going back to a paper map after using GPS.

This is what makes the product defensible. The moat is not the technology. The moat is the accumulated, irreplaceable knowledge the agent has built about you, stored in a format that only works inside this system.

## The Long-Term Vision: The Preference Economy

The product starts as a personal discovery tool. The long-term vision is an open agentic economy where every restaurant, musician, bookseller, and retailer publishes their offerings in a machine-readable format to an open registry. Your agent queries that registry directly, finds the exact match, and completes the transaction without a single ad being served and without Google, Meta, or Amazon taking a cut.

That is the Preference Economy. And this product is the first step toward building it.
